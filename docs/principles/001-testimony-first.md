# Principle 001

## Testimony First

A testimony should not be improved.

A testimony should not be embellished.

A testimony should not be made more poetic.

A testimony should not be given meaning that was not present.

The role of the artist is not to rewrite the testimony.

The role of the artist is to remain faithful to it.

---

## Visual Testimony

The image exists to serve the testimony.

The testimony does not exist to serve the image.

---

## Interpretation

Recognition may emerge later.

Meaning may emerge later.

Theology may emerge later.

But these should not be inserted into the testimony itself.

---

## Guiding Rule

Do not add meaning.

Do not add theology.

Do not add symbolism.

Remain faithful to the testimony.

---

## Closing

The testimony comes first.

Recognition comes later.
