# Visual Testimony 001 — White Light

I was deep beneath the waters of a lake.

Everything was dark.

I could see nothing.

Slowly, I drifted downward toward the lakebed.

Then I noticed something.

A tiny light resting in the sand.

It was small.

Yet it shone with astonishing brightness.

I moved toward it.

As I drew closer, the light became clearer.

I reached out my hand.

And took hold of it.

Then I woke.


## Spatial Structure

Deep beneath the waters of a lake.

Dark water surrounded the dreamer.

The lakebed was visible below.

A tiny light rested in the sand.

The light was small but intensely bright.

The dreamer moved through the water toward the light.
