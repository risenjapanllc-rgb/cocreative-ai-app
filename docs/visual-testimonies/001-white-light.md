# Visual Testimony 001 — White Light

I was deep beneath the waters of a lake.

Everything was dark.

I could see nothing.

Slowly, I drifted downward toward the lakebed.

Then I noticed something.

A tiny light resting in the sand.

It was small.

Yet it shone with astonishing brightness.

I moved toward it.

As I drew closer, the light became clearer.

I reached out my hand.

And took hold of it.

Then I woke.

## Core Witness

In the darkness,
I noticed a tiny light.

Drawn toward it naturally,
I approached it and grasped it.

The certainty of grasping the light remained.
