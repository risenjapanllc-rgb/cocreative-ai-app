# 003 Conversational Presence

## Observation

Users need a gentle invitation.

Not:

"What do you want to say?"

But:

"What remains with you?"

This aligns with the architecture of recognition,
where meaning is often discovered after the experience,
rather than before it.

---

## Experiment

### Question

Can conversation reveal presence?

### Hypothesis

The light does not respond to words.

The light responds to attention.

### Goal

The user begins to notice
what remains with them.
