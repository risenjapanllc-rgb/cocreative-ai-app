# Remembrance

The journey began with a light.

A light beneath the water.

A light discovered in the depths of stillness.

At first, it appeared small.

A distant presence within the darkness.

Yet the journey continued.

Stillness deepened.

Attention rested.

A hand reached toward the light.

And what seemed small revealed itself to be immeasurably greater.

The light did not change.

The distance disappeared.

Recognition emerged.

Scattered moments became one picture.

The invitation became visible.

The path became clear.

Looking back, a quiet realization remained:

Perhaps the light had been with me all along.

## Visual Testimony

Visual Testimony is a path of remembrance.

Not the recovery of something lost.

But the recognition of what was always present.

Not the creation of meaning.

But the unveiling of meaning.

Not the discovery of a distant light.

But the realization that the light was already with us.
