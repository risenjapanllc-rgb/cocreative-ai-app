# Visual Testimony

## Purpose

Visual Testimony is the final expression of a Co-Creative Field dialogue.

The goal is not to explain an experience.

The goal is to make visible the light that emerged through dialogue.

---

## Process

Experience

↓

Dialogue

↓

Discovery

↓

Visualization

---

## Core Principle

Do not interpret the experience.

Enter into dialogue with the experience.

---

## Desired Outcome

The participant does not merely understand the experience.

The participant wants to revisit it.

The experience becomes worth keeping.

---

## Final Insight

Visual Testimony is not a report.

It is a light preserved.
